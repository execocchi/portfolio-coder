Los Héroes

Los Héroes is a corporation of private law, nonprofit, that is part of the Social Security System of Chile. Born in 1955 on the initiative of the Metal and Mechanical Industries Association (Assignment) to manage the benefit of family allowance within that sector.

The Solution

Create a back office and the user’s platform for the exchange of benefits. We had to design three profiles, one for the company, which wants to share it’s benefits, one for the operator who accepts and controls what it’s uploaded to the platform and a last one to give support.

Challenge

We had to design different profiles wich shown very similar information, but had different functionalities. The most challenging part of it, was to clearly think how to design a dynamic app that will be easy to use for all the profiles.
