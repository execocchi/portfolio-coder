The transformative power and importance of economic empowerment cuts across borders, politics, religions, and ethnicities. Further, diverse connections between people from disparate backgrounds will only strengthen our economy.

Clarín was created by Roberto Noble, former minister of the Buenos Aires Province, on 28 August 1945. It was one of the first Argentine newspapers published in tabloid format.

Los Héroes is a corporation of private law, nonprofit, that is part of the Social Security System of Chile. Born in 1955 on the initiative of the Metal and Mechanical Industries Association (Assignment) to manage the benefit of family allowance within that sector.
